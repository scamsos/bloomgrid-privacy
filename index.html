Privacy Policy for BLOOMGRID

Last updated: 13 June 2026

BLOOMGRID ("the app", "we", "us") is a single-player puzzle game. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information the app does and does not collect.

Information We Collect

None. BLOOMGRID does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. The app does not require an account or login, contains no advertising, and includes no third-party analytics, trackers, or advertising identifiers.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your game progress — including scores, Pollen, unlocked themes, island progress, discovered species, statistics, and settings — is saved only in local storage on your device. This data never leaves your device and is removed when you uninstall the app or clear its data.

Permissions

The app may use the device Vibration feature to provide haptic feedback during gameplay. This is used only for in-game feedback and does not collect or transmit any information.

In-App Purchases

If optional in-app purchases (such as premium visual themes) are offered, those transactions are processed by Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment details; that information is handled by Google in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

Children

BLOOMGRID does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

Data Deletion

Because no personal data leaves your device, you can remove all stored data at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
